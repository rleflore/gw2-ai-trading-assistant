# GW2 Item Relationships & Crafting Chains

## Legendary Weapon Generations

All legendary weapons require Mystic Clovers, T6 materials, and generation-specific components.

### Generation 1 (Core Tyria)

Recipe: Gift of Mastery + Gift of Fortune + Legendary Weapon Gift + Precursor Weapon

- **Precursors:** Tradeable on TP. Prices have dropped over time due to Wizard Vault Starter Kits injecting supply.
- **Gift of Fortune:** Requires 250 of each T6 fine material + 250 Globs of Ectoplasm + Mystic Clovers.
- **Gift of Mastery:** Requires Bloodstone Shard, Gift of Exploration, Gift of Battle, Obsidian Shards.

### Generation 2 (Heart of Thorns / Path of Fire)

Recipe: Gift of Maguuma Mastery + Mystic Tribute + Legendary Weapon Gift + Precursor

- **Precursors:** Account-bound. Cannot be traded. Must be crafted via multi-tier collections.
- **Mystic Tribute:** Requires Gift of Condensed Might + Gift of Condensed Magic + Mystic Clovers + large quantities of materials.
- Most expensive and time-consuming generation.

### Generation 3 (End of Dragons)

Recipe: Gift of Jade Mastery + Draconic Tribute + Legendary Weapon Gift + Precursor

- **Precursors:** Tradeable on TP. Crafted via recipes from Levias in Arborstone (Imperial Favors + Research Notes).
- Crafting Gen 3 precursors can be profitable if material costs are low.
- Prices track closely with raw material values.

## Legendary Armor, Trinkets, and Relics

All require Mystic Tribute (same as Gen 2 weapons), plus mode-specific components:
- Raid armor, PvP armor, WvW armor each have different acquisition paths
- Trinkets require Mystic Tribute + map-specific currencies
- New legendary rings require specific materials that spike on release (e.g., Coral Orbs, Ball of Charged Mists Essence)

## Mystic Clover Production

The primary Mystic Coin sink. Every legendary needs 77 Clovers (weapons) or similar quantities.

**Single Gamble:**
- 1 Mystic Coin + 1 Glob of Ectoplasm + 1 Obsidian Shard + 6 Philosopher's Stones
- ~30% chance for 1 Clover. Failed rolls give T6 materials.

**Batch Gamble (10x):**
- 10 Mystic Coins + 10 Ectos + 10 Obsidian Shards + 10 Mystic Crystals
- Same 30% rate but processed in bulk.

**Guaranteed (Wizard Vault):**
- Bypasses gambling entirely using Astral Acclaim.

## Material Promotion (Mystic Forge)

Lower-tier materials can be promoted to higher tiers:

**Basic materials (ore, wood, cloth, leather):**
- 250 lower-tier + Dust + Philosopher's Stones → 40-60 higher-tier
- Used to convert cheap T5 materials into expensive T6.

**Fine materials (blood, bones, venom, etc.):**
- Same promotion mechanic. T5 → T6 is the most common trade.

**Dust promotion:**
- 250 Pile of Incandescent Dust + 1 Pile of Crystalline Dust + 5 Mystic Crystals + 5 Philosopher's Stones → 6-10 Pile of Crystalline Dust.

**Mists Essences:**
- Promoted at 5:1 ratio using Mystic Coin + Ecto + Crystalline Dust.
- Can be demoted at 1:3 ratio using Master's Salvage Kit.

**Spirit Shards** (used to buy Philosopher's Stones and Mystic Crystals):
- Earned passively at level 80 (254,000 XP per shard)
- Random drops, Treasure Mushrooms (HoT/LS3)

## Ascended Material Refinement (Ectoplasm Refinement)

Step 1 (time-gated, once daily):
- T5 basic material + Ecto → raw ascended material (e.g., Lump of Mithrillium)

Step 2 (not time-gated, tradeable):
- Raw ascended material + T2/T3/T4 basic materials → refined ascended material
- Deldrimor Steel Ingot, Bolt of Damask, Elonian Leather Square, Spiritwood Plank

The time gate on Step 1 is what keeps refined ascended material prices high.

## Salvage Chains

- Rare equipment → Globs of Ectoplasm (87.5% chance per salvage with correct kit)
- Exotic equipment → Globs of Ectoplasm + sigils/runes
- Unidentified Gear → identified gear → salvage → materials
- Rare Unidentified Gear is a primary source of Ectos entering the economy

## Key Dependencies (Signal Triggers)

When a patch mentions any of these, check downstream prices:

| If patch affects... | Watch prices of... |
|---|---|
| Legendary crafting recipes | Mystic Coins, T6 mats, Clovers, Ectos |
| New legendary item announced | Specific new materials + all universal legendary mats |
| Salvage rates or drop tables | Ectoplasm, Unidentified Gear |
| Mystic Forge recipes | Mystic Coins, Philosopher's Stones, Spirit Shards |
| Time-gated crafting | Ascended materials (Deldrimor, Damask, etc.) |
| Map reward changes | Map-specific currencies, Volatile Magic |
| Farming meta nerfs/buffs | Materials from that farm, Ectos |
| Festival start | Festival-specific materials, Ectos (Four Winds) |
| Weapon type becomes universal | Existing gear of that type (all stat combos) |
| Class/build nerfs | Gear with that build's preferred stats (sell pressure) |
| Class/build buffs | Gear with buffed build's stats, new BiS runes/sigils |
| New expansion (combat changes) | Weapons/armor newly usable by more classes |
