# GW2 Item Categories for Trading

## Tier 6 Fine Crafting Materials (High Priority)

Universal legendary components. Always in demand. Key watchlist items:

- Vial of Powerful Blood
- Powerful Venom Sac
- Elaborate Totem
- Pile of Crystalline Dust
- Armored Scale
- Ancient Bone
- Vicious Fang
- Vicious Claw

These are required in quantities of 250 for Gift of Fortune / Gift of Condensed Might / Gift of Condensed Magic. Prices move together as a group when legendary demand shifts.

## Tier 5 Fine Crafting Materials

Primary inputs for Mystic Forge promotion to T6:

- Vial of Potent Blood
- Potent Venom Sac
- Intricate Totem
- Pile of Incandescent Dust
- Large Scale
- Large Bone
- Large Fang
- Large Claw

Promotion recipe: 250 T5 + Dust + Philosopher's Stones → 40-60 T6. Price relationship: T5 should be ~1/5th to 1/4th of T6 price when promotion is profitable.

## Universal Legendary Materials (Highest Volatility)

- **Mystic Coins:** Most liquid and traded material. Used in Clover gambling, direct recipes, and dozens of Mystic Forge recipes. Primary watchlist item.
- **Globs of Ectoplasm:** Extremely volatile. Affected by salvage rates, farms, festivals, and Mystic Forge recipes. Second most important watchlist item.
- **Obsidian Shards:** Always in demand for Clover gambling and various recipes.
- **Amalgamated Gemstones:** Spike with any expansion or content update requiring them.

## Ascended Crafting Materials (Tier 7)

Time-gated daily refinement creates price floors:

- **Deldrimor Steel Ingot** (metal)
- **Bolt of Damask** (cloth)
- **Elonian Leather Square** (leather)
- **Spiritwood Plank** (wood)

Prices are structurally elevated due to time gates. Spike when new ascended/legendary recipes are introduced.

## Basic Crafting Materials (Tiered)

Raw → Refined pipeline. Tiers 1-6:

**Metal:** Copper Ore → Iron Ore → Gold Ore → Platinum Ore → Mithril Ore → Orichalcum Ore
**Wood:** Green Wood Log → Soft Wood Log → Seasoned Wood Log → Hard Wood Log → Elder Wood Log → Ancient Wood Log
**Cloth:** Jute Scrap → Wool Scrap → Cotton Scrap → Linen Scrap → Silk Scrap → Gossamer Scrap
**Leather:** Rawhide → Thin → Coarse → Rugged → Thick → Hardened Leather Section

T5 and T6 are highest value. Lower tiers used in ascended refinement Step 2.

## Precursor Weapons

### Gen 1 (Tradeable, prices declining over time)
- Dawn, Dusk, The Legend, Spark, Storm, etc.
- Supply constantly increased by Wizard Vault Starter Kits
- Watch for: supply injections (price drops), temporary removal from vault (price recovery)

### Gen 2 (Account-bound, not tradeable)
- Cannot be traded. No direct TP signal.
- Indirectly affect material prices (crafting them consumes huge quantities of basic materials)

### Gen 3 (Tradeable, tracks material costs)
- Dragon's Fang, Dragon's Wisdom, etc.
- Prices closely follow raw material values
- Profitable to craft when material costs dip below TP price

## Mists Essences (Advanced)

Used in Mystic Forge recipes and legendary crafting:

- Shard of Mists Essence (lowest tier)
- Fragment of Mists Essence
- Glob of Mists Essence
- Ball of Mists Essence
- Ball of Charged Mists Essence (highest tier, ~400g+ normally)

Promotion: 5:1 ratio using Mystic Coin + Ecto + Crystalline Dust.
Demotion: 1:3 ratio using Master's Salvage Kit.

Extremely volatile when new recipes require specific tiers.

## Seasonal & Event Materials

Predictable annual cycles:

- **Trick-or-Treat Bags** (Halloween): Spike before, stabilize during
- **Wintersday Gifts** (December): Supply flood during event
- **Zhaitaffy** (Dragon Bash): Consumable demand
- **Zephyrite Supply Boxes** (Four Winds): Drives Ecto demand
- **Candy Corn** (Halloween): Bulk material, price cycles annually

## Salvage Economy Items

- **Rare Unidentified Gear:** Primary Ecto source. Price tracks Ecto value minus salvage kit cost.
- **Exotic Unidentified Gear:** Higher value salvage, sigils/runes output.
- **Masterwork Unidentified Gear:** Bulk material source (cloth, leather, metal).
- **Trophy Shipments:** Purchased with Volatile Magic. Floods baseline materials.

## Map-Specific & Expansion Materials

- **Volatile Magic:** Converted to Trophy Shipments; controls baseline material supply.
- **Imperial Favors:** Used for Gen 3 precursor recipes.
- **Research Notes:** Crafted from salvaging crafted items. Used for Gen 3 recipes.
- **Antique Summoning Stones:** Time-gated/vendor-bought. Used in EoD legendary crafting.
- **Memory of Aurene:** EoD/SotO meta-event currency for newer legendaries.
- **Fresh Winterberries:** LS3 currency, easily farmed, used for various conversions.

## Consumables (Steady Demand)

- Ascended food and utility items (raids, fractals, WvW)
- Tuning Crystals
- Specific stat-food recipes that require niche ingredients

High daily volume, relatively stable prices, but supply-side changes (recipe modifications) can cause spikes.

## Signal Priority Ranking

When generating trading signals, prioritize monitoring:

1. Mystic Coins (universal indicator)
2. Globs of Ectoplasm (supply/demand barometer)
3. T6 Fine Materials (legendary demand proxy)
4. Newly-required materials (from patch notes)
5. Ascended materials (time-gated stability)
6. Seasonal materials (predictable cycles)
7. Gen 1/Gen 3 Precursors (market sentiment)
